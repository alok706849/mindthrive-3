My Story

From Struggle
to Strength

I have a deep appreciation of how inner unrest can impact our lives — mine played out as eating disorders, depression, massive insecurity, low self esteem and anxiety.

During those decades, I consulted doctors, psychiatrists, psychologists, counsellors, naturopaths and nutritionists. They all helped, but they didn't give me the deep-seated change I needed.

The turning point was when I had to walk away from a business I had created, experiencing panic attacks and mentally imploding from the stress. That cost me $50k, 2 years of my life, a friendship and my confidence.

I decided to see a life coach — and that changed everything. It was the catalyst for me to train and grow as a coach. Fast forward to today: I am a qualified NLP Practitioner, Counsellor, Life Coach and Breathwork Facilitator — and creator of MindThrive.
